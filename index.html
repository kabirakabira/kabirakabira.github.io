Latest Project

The Lower West Side (Pilsen): History, Demographics, and Gentrification

During the Fall '24 semester at UIC, I prepared this memorandum on Pilsen as a part of the course UPP 501: Space, Place, and Institutions. This memorandum looks at the history, demographics, and physical structure of the Lower West Side and incorporates stakeholder interviews to identify reasons for and indicators of gentrification in the neighborhood.
