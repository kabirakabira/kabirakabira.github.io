Demographics in Reach

06/2023

For some projects, Census geographies (counties, tracts, etc) might not be optimal. This application takes a user-inputted point (XY coordinates) and pulls a set of demographic data for the area within a specified walk or drive time. This application uses the OSRM library to create an isochrone, then intersects the isochrone with Census tracts and pulls and summarizes demographic data for the area covered by the isochrone. The data can be downloaded as a geopackage.

RE: Investigating the Deterrent Effect of Capital Punishment on Violent Crime Rates

12/2020

I revisited my earlier project on the same topic, with an updated methodology and research design. With this design, I utilize individual executions in U.S. States as an exogenous shock to crime rates and implement a difference-in-differences (DID) strategy as well as a regression discontinuity (RD) strategy. In line with previous research in this field, I found that capital punishmnent does not have any discernible effect on violent crime rates.
